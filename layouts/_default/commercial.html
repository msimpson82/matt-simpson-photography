Discovery

Common Questions
FAQ
What kind of commercial photography do you offer?

Product photography, lifestyle and brand photography, restaurant and food photography, corporate and industrial photography, and full marketing campaign shoots. If your business needs professional images, I can handle it.

How much does commercial photography cost?

Pricing depends on the scope of the project — what you're shooting, how long the shoot takes, and what deliverables you need. Reach out for a custom quote.

Do I get full usage rights to the photos?

Yes. You receive full usage rights to all delivered images for your business. Use them on your website, social media, print materials, advertising, and anywhere else you need them.

How long does a commercial shoot take?

It depends on the project. A product shoot might take a few hours. A full lifestyle or campaign shoot could be a full day or more. We plan the timeline together before the shoot so you know exactly what to expect.

Can you shoot at my business location?

Yes. I shoot on-location at offices, warehouses, restaurants, retail spaces, and anywhere else your business operates. I bring my own lighting and equipment and adapt to whatever the space requires.

Do you work with advertising agencies?

Yes. I've worked with agencies including Omythic, Price Weber, and Makespace Web on campaigns for their clients. I understand agency workflows, creative briefs, and deliverable requirements.

Where are you located?

My studio is in the Germantown neighborhood of Louisville, KY. I shoot on-location throughout Louisville, Southern Indiana (Jeffersonville, New Albany, Clarksville), Lexington, KY, and surrounding areas.

How quickly will I get my photos?

Turnaround time depends on the scope of the project and is discussed at booking. I respect deadlines and always give you a clear timeline upfront.
