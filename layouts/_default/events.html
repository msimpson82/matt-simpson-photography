Common Questions
FAQ
How far in advance should I book event photography?

As early as possible. For large events, conferences, and festivals, I recommend reaching out at least 4 to 6 weeks ahead. For smaller events, 2 weeks is usually fine, but the sooner you lock it in, the better.

How long will you be at my event?

That depends on the event. Some need 2 hours of coverage, others need a full day. We discuss the schedule beforehand so you get exactly the coverage you need.

Do you bring your own lighting?

Yes. I carry professional lighting gear and use it whenever the venue needs it. Convention floors, ballrooms, outdoor evening events — I'm equipped for all of it.

How many photos will I receive?

It depends on the length and scale of the event. A 2-hour corporate event might deliver 100 to 200 images. A full-day festival could be 500 or more. Every image is edited and ready to use.

Can I use the photos for marketing and social media?

Yes. You receive full usage rights for all delivered images. Use them on your website, social media, printed materials, and future event promotions.

Do you shoot video at events?

My focus is photography. If you need video, I can recommend trusted videographers in Louisville who I've worked alongside at events.

What does event photography cost?

Pricing depends on the type of event, duration, and deliverables. Reach out through the contact form for a quote tailored to your event.

Where do you cover events?

I cover events throughout Louisville, KY, Southern Indiana (Jeffersonville, New Albany, Clarksville), Lexington, KY, and surrounding areas within about 60 miles.
