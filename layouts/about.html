About Matt

Louisville, KY photographer specializing in headshots, events, and commercial photography.

I'm Matt Simpson, a commercial photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky with over a decade of experience. I specialize in professional headshots, personal branding photography, and event coverage for businesses, organizations, and individuals throughout the Louisville metro and Southern Indiana area.

Over the years, I've worked with everyone from small startups to major brands, shooting everything from celebrity chefs to hometown heroes. While my roots are in food and beverage photography, my focus has shifted toward editorial work — portraits, documentary storytelling, and fine art.

Whether you need polished corporate headshots for your team, branding photos that tell your company's story, or coverage of your next conference or event, I bring a versatile eye and a relaxed approach that puts people at ease in front of the camera.

My studio is in the Germantown neighborhood of Louisville. I shoot on-location throughout Louisville, Southern Indiana (Jeffersonville, New Albany, Clarksville), and Lexington, KY.

Based in Louisville, KY and available for travel. Let's work together.
