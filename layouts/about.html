My name is Matt Simpson and I've been a professional photographer for over 10 years. I've worked with small businesses to big, bad ass brands. I've shot celebrity chefs and hometown heroes. While most people know me as a food and beverage photographer, this site represents my shift towards editorial photography with a focus on portrait work, documentary, and fine art.

You'll also find my collection of photography for hire services, of course. Galleries that represent my work in event photography, weddings, commercial, branding, and professional headshots.

If you have a need for a photographer who can work in a multitude of genres, I'd love to help.
