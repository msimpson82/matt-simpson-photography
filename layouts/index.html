About the Photographer

Who's Behind the Camera

I'm Matt Simpson, a commercial photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky. I work with businesses, executives, and organizations that need professional photography — headshots, events, and commercial campaigns — and I've been doing it for years.

My studio is in the Germantown neighborhood of Louisville. I shoot on-location throughout Louisville, Southern Indiana (Jeffersonville, New Albany, Clarksville), and Lexington, KY. I've worked with CEOs, marketing teams, startups, and organizations of all sizes. My images end up on websites, LinkedIn, billboards, brochures, and marketing campaigns.
